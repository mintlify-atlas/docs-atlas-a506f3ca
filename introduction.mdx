## What is RespondeIA?

RespondeIA is a SaaS platform that automates customer service on WhatsApp using artificial intelligence. It handles inquiries, schedules appointments, and closes sales automatically—without requiring code or technical knowledge.

**Ready to start?** You can have your AI chatbot live and responding to customers in under 10 minutes.
</Info>

### Automatic Appointment Scheduling

The assistant manages appointments and bookings directly in WhatsApp conversations. Customers can view available slots and book instantly.

<Note>
The bot handles the entire booking flow—from showing available times to confirming appointments—without human intervention.
</Note>

### Integrated CRM

Centralized contact registry with complete conversation history. Track all customer interactions in one place.

### Real-Time Analytics

Monitor resolution rates and customer satisfaction from a unified dashboard. Get insights into bot performance and customer behavior.

### Controlled Handoff to Human Agents

When a query requires human attention, the system transfers the conversation with full context. Your team sees the entire conversation history.

### MercadoPago Integration

Accept payments directly in WhatsApp conversations. The newest integration allows you to process payments without leaving the chat.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with email and password, or continue with Google. Registration takes less than 2 minutes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect WhatsApp">
    Migrate your existing WhatsApp number to the official Meta API, or get a new number. The process is guided and takes about 10 minutes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Train Your AI">
    Provide information about your business, products, and services. The AI learns your business context.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Activate your bot and start responding to customer messages automatically. Monitor performance from your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

```json
// Tech stack from package.json
```
